Meet Point

Point listens to your home and senses what's in the air. It tells you about things you care about, like windows breaking, alarms sounding, or the presence of smoke. It’s peace of mind without cameras. Created by a Scandinavian team with long experience from Apple. Point is now available on Kickstarter.

Our purpose

Form was founded on the principle that peace of mind shouldn't come at the expense of privacy. Form designs are simple and unbranded in Scandinavian tradition.

Founder Nils Mattisson, with long experience from Apple, is joined by an impressive team with backgrounds from the Nordic telecom giants. Together they have years of experience building and shipping hardware and software products.

Form has established manufacturing operations in Shenzhen, China and is scheduled to ship its first product, Point, early next year.

The company has operations in the San Francisco, CA, Malmö, Sweden and Shenzhen, China.

Team

Founder and Apple alum Nils Mattisson has brought together a Scandinavian team with an eye for electronics, software and design.


Nils Mattisson

Nils joined Apple in 2006 to develop prototypes of new ideas before they became products. In 2013 he left Apple to found Form. Nils enjoys the outdoors, has a past as a mountain guide and is happiest riding his bicycle.


Kristian Dupont

With a background in computer science from Copenhagen University, Kristian has 20 years of experience developing software of all kinds. Recently he's been travelling the world while engaging in several entrepreneurial ventures.


Marcus Ljungblad

Marcus specializes in distributed systems and studied CS at the Royal Institue of Technology in Stockholm. Previously he did research on energy efficient buildings at Malmö University. When he is not at his computer, he might be outside with the Scouts.


Fredrik Ahlberg

Fredrik is an electronics and embedded systems hacker who has worked at research departments of Ericsson and Axis Communications. He also plays the trombone and enjoys outdoor life, though not necessarily at the same time.


Martin Lööf

Having studied at China's top university Zhejing, Martin brings experience in mechatronics, manufacturing and Chinese. He enjoys taking things apart and spends his free time kitesurfing.

History

Fall 2013: Nils leaves Apple and starts working on Form.

Fall 2013 - Spring 2014: First prototype of Point is developed at Betahaus, a European coworking space for startups.

Summmer 2014: Joined Haxlr8r and moved to Shenzhen, China, to develop a production-ready prototype.

November 2014: Point launches on Kickstarter.
