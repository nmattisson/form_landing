About Us

Form is a team of Apple alumni, seasoned entrepreneurs and former security professionals with combined decades of experience in designing, developing and manufacturing consumer electronics at scale. We have been in Shenzhen for months as part of the Haxlr8r program, the premier hardware accelerator in the world, preparing for manufacturing. By being in China and working with the factories long before our launch we have a head start on manufacturing and logistics before our Kickstarter campaign.

Team

Founder and Apple alum Nils Mattisson has brought together a Scandinavian team with an eye for electronics, software and design.


Nils Mattisson

Nils joined Apple in 2006 to develop prototypes of new ideas before they became products. In 2013 he left Apple to found Form. Nils enjoys the outdoors, has a past as a mountain guide and is happiest riding his bicycle.


Kristian Dupont

With a background in computer science from Copenhagen University, Kristian has 20 years of experience developing software of all kinds. Recently he's been travelling the world while engaging in several entrepreneurial ventures.


Marcus Ljungblad

Marcus specializes in distributed systems and studied CS at the Royal Institue of Technology in Stockholm. Previously he did research on energy efficient buildings at Malmö University. When he is not at his computer, he might be outside with the Scouts.


Fredrik Ahlberg

Fredrik is an electronics and embedded systems hacker who has worked at research departments of Ericsson and Axis Communications. He also plays the trombone and enjoys outdoor life, though not necessarily at the same time.


Martin Lööf

Having studied at China's top university, the University of Zhejiang, Martin brings experience in mechatronics, manufacturing and Chinese. He enjoys taking things apart and spends his free time kitesurfing.

  • Summer 2013

    Leaving Apple

    The story of Form starts in California. Founder Nils Mattisson saw the Internet of Things taking off and the hardware renaissance established. Product designers and hardware engineers no longer needed to work in large corporations to bring ideas to market.

  • Fall 2013 - Spring 2014

    Betahaus

    Because nothing is as conducive to innovation as a good community, Mattisson started working out of Betahaus, a coworking space with locations across Europe. There he put together a team of entreprenuers and makers. Now calling ourselves Form, we presented Point at Betapitch, a competition for hardware startups in Berlin, and found ourselves on the podium.

  • Summer 2014

    Haxlr8r

    As much as we love Europe, there is no place in the world for making hardware like Shenzhen. It's like a city-size Techshop and the prototyping environment is lightning-fast. We were honored to be accepted to Hax this summer and it's been a defining time for Form, sharpening our ideas and preparing us for manufacturing.

  • Winter 2014

    Launch

    We're preparing for the big day in November when we will take the veil off and launch our Kickstarter campaign. If you want to follow our journey, sign up to our newsletter where we post updates as we go!
