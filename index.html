Introducing Form

Home is more than four walls and a place to sleep. It’s a place where we feel comfortable and safe. A place for the people we love and the things we cherish.

Sometimes when we travel we wonder if everything is alright at home. We don't want to install invasive home security systems that force us to make choices between privacy and peace of mind.

This winter Form will introduce Point, offering home security without surveillance.

Point is an internet-enabled house sitter. It measures the indoor environment and notices sharp noises. Its highly sensitive particle detector picks up on common home hazards and things like cigarette smoke.

It’s perfect for AirBnB hosts and others who don’t want to intrude on the privacy of their guests yet be notified if something goes wrong.

Fasttrack to Trust

We trust that our guests treat our homes with respect, but accidents happen. It's easy to forget the stove on or a window open in a new environment. With point you can trust that if these things do happen you'll know and your guest can get a reminder. It's a fasttrack to trust.

Heartbeat from Home

Most of us don't want full home security systems. We live in apartment buildings or rent our homes in safe neighbourhoods. Still, being reassured in the knowledge that if something went wrong we'd know allows us to relax when we're travelling. We call it Heartbeat from Home.

Security without Surveillance

Many of us are uneasy with security cameras at home. We don't want to be able to know our spouse's every move nor have the ability to invade on our friends or guests privacy. We might not even want a video stream of our homes available in the cloud at all.

About Us

Form is a team of Apple alumni, seasoned entrepreneurs and former security professionals with combined decades of experience in designing, developing and manufacturing consumer electronics at scale. We have been in Shenzhen for months as part of the Haxlr8r program, the premier hardware accelerator in the world, preparing for manufacturing. By being in China and working with the factories long before our launch we have a head start on manufacturing and logistics before our crowdfunding campaign.

  • Summer 2013

    Leaving Apple

    The story of Form starts in California. Internet of Things had taken off and the hardware renaissance established. No longer needed product designers and hardware engineers work in large corporations to bring ideas to market.

  • Fall 2013 - Spring 2014

    Betahaus

    Nothing is as conducive to innovation as a good community, so we started working out of Betahaus, a coworking space for entreprenuers and makers with locations across Europe. We presented Form for the first time at Betapitch, a competition for hardware startups in Berlin, and found ourselves on the podium.

  • Summer 2014

    Haxlr8r

    As much as we love Europe, there is no place in the world for making hardware like Shenzhen. It's like a city-size Techshop and the prototyping environment is lightning-fast. We were honored to be accepted to Hax this summer and it's been a defining time for Form, sharpening our ideas and preparing for manufacturing.

    Launch

    We're preparing for the big day when we will take the veil off and launch our Kickstarter campaign. If you want to follow our journey, sign up to our newsletter where we post updates as we go!
