Help Friends Out

If you have a spare moment, here is a couple ways in which you can help Form. As a small and fledgling company just taking our first few steps into the world, your support is incredibly important to us!

1. Vote for us on Engadget

We're participating in Engadget's Insert Coin Competition. Cast your vote for Form! Sometimes the site is a bit buggy when used with adblock extensions, so consider turning it off for a moment and then do the following:

  1. Go to the voting page.
  2. Scroll down to 'Point' and click the Vote Now button.
  3. Vote once with Twitter and then repeat for Facebook. Nothing will be posted to your account.

2. Will you like Us?

Internet is a popularity contest and right now we're looking every bit as popular as we did in High School.
